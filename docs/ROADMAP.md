# Roadmap

## v0.2 — Player Progression

- Per-player XP / level system
- Stat growth tied to usage (playing tournaments improves relevant stats organically)
- Potential stat ceiling that rises with level

## v0.3 — Scouting

- Scout pool of unsigned players
- Recruitment costs money + reputation
- Players have hidden potential stats revealed by scouting

## v0.4 — Sponsors

- Sponsors offer contracts based on club reputation
- Provide passive income or one-time bonuses
- May set performance goals (top-X finish, rep threshold)

## v0.5 — Aging System

- Players have age affecting peak and decline trajectory
- Retirement mechanic
- Youth academy / development pipeline

## v0.6 — Richer Tournament Variety

- More course types with distinct terrain profiles
- Weather modifiers (wind, rain) affecting simulation
- Special event tournaments (invitational, national championship)

## Backlog (unscheduled)

- Per-disc/per-player stat breakdown UI
- Club facility upgrades (training center, disc lab)
- Rival clubs with their own rosters and progression
- Multi-season historical records + trophy room
